How to Play

Direct the snake towards the food (red square) on screen using the arrow keys. Once the food is reached, a point is scored and a new block is added to the current snake. Then, another block of food will appear at a random location on screen. The game ends when the head of the snake either moves into the border or into itself. The goal is to make the snake as large as possible before that happens.
